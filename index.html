Expert Doctor Team, More than 19 Years of Experience

Shifa Hospital Group was the first Turkish hospital to become JCI (Joint Commission International) accredited, the gold standard in health care. With 11 highly technological and patient-oriented hospitals and 2 medical centers and 1 wellness center in 5 Turkish cities, the group serve 75,000 international patients from +167 countries annually.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Does the hospital offer any specialty services?

    Yes, in addition to our general medical and surgical services, we also have specialized departments for cardiology, oncology, orthopedics, and women's health.

  • What should I do in an emergency?

    In the event of a medical emergency, please call 911 or go directly to our emergency department. Our team is available 24/7 to provide immediate care.

  • What are the visiting hours for patients?

    Visiting hours are from 9 AM to 8 PM daily. Visitors are asked to check in at the front desk upon arrival.

  • How do I make an appointment with a doctor?

    You can make an appointment by calling our scheduling line at (123) 456-7890 or by using our online scheduling portal on our website.

  • What should I bring to my first appointment?

    Please bring your insurance card, a valid ID, and any relevant medical records or test results. This will help us provide the best care for you.
